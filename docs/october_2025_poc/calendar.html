Community Calendar

A consolidated view of island events, maintained by our community.

Island Events at a Glance

This calendar combines events from participating organizations on Thetis Island. Each group maintains its own schedule; updates and changes appear here automatically. You can click an event for details or use the tabs at the top to switch views.

Need to add your group’s events? Please contact ThetisIslandCalendar@gmail.com for instructions on sharing your calendar, or use the submission form if your group doesn’t have one.
