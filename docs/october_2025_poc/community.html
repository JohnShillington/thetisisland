Community Organizations & Groups

Discover the groups and associations that enrich life on Thetis Island.

Organizations

These organizations have dedicated websites and manage their own programs. Follow the links below to learn more or get involved.

  • Forbes Hall Community Centre (TICA) – The Thetis Island Community Association manages Forbes Hall and hosts events, programs and a community calendar. Visit forbeshall.ca.
  • Thetis Island Community Fund (TICF) – Providing financial support to local initiatives. Learn more at thetisislandcommunityfund.ca.
  • Thetis Island Volunteer Fire Department (TIVFD) – Fire protection and first responder services. See thetisislandfire.ca.
  • Thetis Island Nature Conservancy (ThINC) – Conserving and stewarding natural habitats. Visit thetisislandnatureconservancy.org.
  • Islands Trust – Thetis Island – Local planning authority for land use and zoning. See the Thetis page at islandstrust.bc.ca.
  • Thetis Island School (TIES) – K‑7 elementary school. For school information, visit sd79.bc.ca and search for Thetis Island.

Clubs & Volunteer Groups

These volunteer‑run groups enrich island life. Contact them directly to participate or support their efforts.

  • Thetis Island Marine Association (TIMA) – Manages community moorage and marine facilities. Contact info@tima.ca.
  • ARCTIC (Amateur Radio Club of Thetis Island) – Supporting emergency radio communications and amateur radio hobbyists. Contact arctic@thetisisland.net.
  • Food Bank – Providing food support for residents in need. Contact myrna.brownlee@gmail.com for assistance or to donate.
  • Friends of Furries – Volunteer group helping island pets and their people. Contact friendsoffurries@gmail.com to get involved or request help.
  • Serenity Group – A meeting group offering support for those affected by addiction. Contact serenity@thetisisland.net for meeting times.
  • Thetis Island Library – Volunteer‑run library located at Forbes Hall. Contact library@thetisisland.net for hours and catalogue.

If your club or organization isn’t listed, please let us know via our contact page so we can add you.
