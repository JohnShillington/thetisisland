title: Community Calendar
description: Combined community calendar for Thetis Island events.
---

A consolidated view of island events, maintained by our community. Each group maintains its own schedule; updates and changes appear here automatically.

## Submit an Event

If your group doesn't have its own calendar, you can submit events to be added to the community calendar. Contact **ThetisIslandCalendar@gmail.com** with your event details.

## Add Your Organization's Calendar

If your organization maintains a Google Calendar and would like it included in the combined community view, please contact **ThetisIslandCalendar@gmail.com** for instructions on sharing your calendar with the Thetis Island aggregator account.
